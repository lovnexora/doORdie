# 💀 DoOrDie

**The productivity app for those who treat deadlines like life and death.**

Most productivity apps give you a "gentle reminder." **DoOrDie** gives you an ultimatum. Designed for the chronic procrastinator, this app turns your To-Do list into a high-stakes survival game where the only options are completion or consequence.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

* **The Ultimatum Timer:** Set a hard deadline. Once the clock starts, there is no "snooze." 
* **Stake Mode:** Trigger consequences (locking apps, alerts, or stakes) if a task isn't marked complete.
* **Burn List:** Tasks that aren't completed by the deadline vanish forever.
* **Aggressive Notifications:** Escalating alerts that demand your attention.
* **Minimalist UI:** Zero distractions. Just you, the clock, and the work.
